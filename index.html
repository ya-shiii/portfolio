03 / ABOUT

BRIDGING INTERFACES AND BACKEND SYSTEMS.

I'm Joshua Vicente, a Full Stack Developer based in the Philippines. I started in design and front-end development, but I became increasingly interested in what happens behind the interface—how data moves, how business workflows are structured, and how different parts of a system work together. Today, I build full-stack applications using Django, Django REST Framework, Nuxt.js, Vue.js, and PostgreSQL. I've worked with local and international clients on e-commerce platforms, event systems, administrative tools, and custom business applications. Outside development, I previously taught Information Technology and currently help lead TECHSAVVY Community in Misamis Occidental. I've also been invited to speak at technology and educational events. I like building software people actually use.
