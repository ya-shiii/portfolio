// OVERVIEW

Kathie's Kitchen is a custom e-commerce platform developed for an international client. It provides customers with a responsive online product browsing and ordering experience while giving the business custom administrative tools for managing products and orders.

// THE PROBLEM

The business needed a dedicated online ordering platform that could present its products clearly, collect customer orders, and reduce the manual work involved in managing order information.

// THE SOLUTION

Built a Nuxt.js storefront connected to Django REST Framework APIs and a PostgreSQL-backed order system. The platform supports product browsing, cart and order submission workflows, administrative order management, and transactional email communication.
